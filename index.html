About

I lead the New Lineages of Life Group at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (DOE JGI/LBNL), where we discover novel bacterial, archaeal, eukaryotic microbes, and viruses in environmental sequence data.

My work combines cutting-edge machine learning with multi-omics approaches to expand our understanding of microbial diversity and develop pathogen-agnostic detection systems for biosecurity applications.

As Biosecurity Lead at DOE JGI, I translate fundamental microbiome research into practical tools for threat detection and characterization.
