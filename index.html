01 Profile
  • New Lineages of Life Group
  • DOE JGI · LBNL
  • Berkeley, California

I lead the New Lineages of Life Group at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (DOE JGI / LBNL), where we discover novel bacterial, archaeal, and eukaryotic microbes — and the viruses that shape them — hidden in environmental sequence data.

My work pairs machine learning with multi-omics to expand our map of microbial diversity and to build pathogen-agnostic detection systems for biosecurity.

As Biosecurity Lead at DOE JGI, I translate fundamental microbiome research into practical tools for threat detection and characterization.
